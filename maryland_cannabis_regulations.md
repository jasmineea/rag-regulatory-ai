## Licensing Basics
Maryland cannabis businesses must be licensed by the Maryland Cannabis Administration (MCA).
Licensees must comply with operational requirements and reporting obligations.

## Advertising and Marketing
Marketing may not target individuals under the legal age.
Advertising restrictions may apply to placement, audience, and content.

## Packaging and Labeling
Products must be sold in packaging designed to reduce access by children.
Labels must include required consumer information and warnings.

## Product Testing
Cannabis products must be tested by authorized laboratories.
Testing covers safety and compliance requirements.

## Recordkeeping and Audits
Licensees must maintain records related to inventory, sales, and operations.
Records may be subject to inspection or audit.
